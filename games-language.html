This project explores how games can be leveraged as a medium for language learning — investigating engagement patterns, learning outcomes, and the intersection of play and pedagogy through a UX research lens.

The team investigated existing gamified language learning tools, conducted user research, and synthesised findings into actionable design recommendations for more effective and engaging learning experiences.
